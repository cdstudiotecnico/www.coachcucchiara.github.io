About Me.

Silence. Run.™
Few words. A lot of running. Above all: Discipline.

My name is Dario Cucchiara, born in 1987. I live in Sicily and my love for running began in 2016.

Trained by Professor Tommaso Ticali, I improved from a 1h45 Half Marathon to a 1h13 personal best at the Lisbon Half Marathon.

I am a FIDAL Certified Instructor, engineer, mathematics and physics teacher, and technical manager for engineering projects.

Running completely changed my life. It taught me discipline, balance, consistency and awareness.

Today I coach amateur and competitive athletes through a structured online coaching method based on heart-rate training, continuous feedback and sustainable progression.

The Method.

I do not simply create schedules. I build discipline, awareness and long-term performance.

Every athlete receives personalized training based on real goals, lifestyle, available time and current fitness level.

Most sessions are built around heart-rate ranges, helping athletes improve performance while protecting recovery and consistency.

The method includes aerobic runs, intervals, long runs, strength work and constant monitoring.

Athletes send daily feedback, allowing continuous adjustments and highly personalized coaching.

Silence. Run. means: less noise, more awareness, more discipline, more authenticity.
