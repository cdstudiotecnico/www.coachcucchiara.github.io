3D Keychain

The first object in the line was created in collaboration with LayerX3DPrint: a light, durable and carefully detailed keychain, made with 3D technology and designed for those who want to carry their way of living running with them.

A small object.
A clear message.

The Silence. Run. keychain is designed to accompany your day: on your keys, in your backpack, in your sports bag or next to the objects you use most often. It is a concrete detail, but it tells a precise choice.

Runners know it well: consistency is not seen only in kilometers. It is also seen in gestures, habits and in the way you stay faithful to the road you have chosen.
