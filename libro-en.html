About Me.

Silence. Run.™
Few words, a lot of running and above all: Discipline.

My name is Dario Cucchiara, born in 1987. I live in Sicily and my love for running began in 2016.

Trained by Professor Tommaso Ticali, I went from running a half marathon in 1h45 to reaching a personal best of 1h13 at the Lisbon Half Marathon.

I am a FIDAL Certified Instructor, an engineer, a mathematics and physics teacher, and a technical manager in my professional field.

Running deeply changed my life. It taught me discipline, balance, consistency and awareness.

Today I coach amateur and competitive runners through a structured online coaching method based on heart-rate training, continuous monitoring and sustainable progression.

The Method.

I do not simply create training plans. I build discipline, awareness and sustainable results.

Every athlete receives a personalized program based on real goals, lifestyle, available time and current fitness level.

Most sessions are structured using heart-rate ranges, in order to improve performance while protecting recovery and consistency.

The method includes aerobic runs, specific workouts, intervals, hills, long runs, strength training and constant monitoring.

Athletes send daily feedback, which allows continuous adjustments and truly personalized coaching.

Silence. Run. means: less noise, more listening, more discipline, more authenticity.

The Book.

Silence. Run. is not simply a book about running. It is an invitation to listen, choose and become.

A journey into running, discipline, silence and presence.

A book for runners, coaches, teachers, parents and anyone searching for deeper meaning.
