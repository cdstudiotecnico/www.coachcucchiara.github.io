My first book.
A journey through running, discipline, silence and presence.
LISTEN. CHOOSE. BECOME.

Silence. Run. is not just a running book. It is an invitation to stop, breathe and truly listen again to what really matters.

In a world filled with noise, speed, expectations and distractions, running becomes much more than a sport performance: it becomes an inner space where clarity, discipline, balance and presence can return.

Through personal experiences, deep reflections and years spent between sport, teaching, coaching and real life, Dario Cucchiara guides the reader into an intense journey inside the true meaning of running.

Page after page, Silence. Run. tells a different vision of sport: more human, more conscious and more authentic. Running is not described only as fatigue, pace or result, but as a way to know yourself, respect time, protect the mind and slowly build a stronger and more present version of yourself.

The real goal is not only to run faster.
It is to learn how to live more deeply.

This book intertwines heart-rate based coaching, discipline without obsession, the importance of recovery, family, teaching, balance, sustainability and the constant search for presence.

Not a manual.

It is not just an autobiography. It is a book for runners, coaches, teachers, workers, parents and for anyone who falls and rises again.

It is a journey.

An invitation for those who feel that life cannot always be lived in a rush, without ever truly listening to it.

With a direct, emotional and reflective style, Silence. Run. speaks to runners, athletes, coaches, students and anyone who wants to rediscover the value of silence, discipline and slow constructions.
